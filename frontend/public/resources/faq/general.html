General FAQ

How often should I visit the dentist?

We recommend visiting the dentist every six months for a checkup and cleaning.

What should I do if I have a dental emergency?

Call our office immediately. If it's after hours, follow the instructions on our voicemail for emergency care.

Do you accept my insurance?

We accept most major dental insurance plans. Please see our Accepted Insurance Plans page or call us to confirm.

How do I book an appointment?

You can book online through our website or call our office during business hours.
