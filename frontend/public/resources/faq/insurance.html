Insurance FAQ

Do you accept my insurance?

We accept most major dental insurance plans. Please see our Accepted Insurance Plans page or call us to confirm.

Will you file my insurance claim?

Yes, we will file your insurance claim as a courtesy to you.

What if my insurance doesn't cover the full cost?

You are responsible for any portion not covered by your insurance. We offer payment plans and financing options to help.

Can I pay with HSA or FSA?

Yes, we accept Health Savings Accounts (HSA) and Flexible Spending Accounts (FSA) for payment.
