After-Hours Emergency Care

If you have a dental emergency outside of our regular office hours, please call our main number at (555) 123-4567 and follow the instructions to reach the on-call dentist.

We are committed to providing prompt care for urgent dental needs, even after hours. If you are unable to reach us and your situation is life-threatening, call 911 or go to the nearest emergency room.

Common after-hours emergencies include severe tooth pain, swelling, trauma, or bleeding that cannot be controlled.
