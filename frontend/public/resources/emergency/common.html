Common Dental Emergencies

  • Toothache: Rinse your mouth with warm water and use dental floss to remove any debris. If pain persists, contact us.
  • Knocked-out tooth: Keep the tooth moist and try to place it back in the socket. If not possible, put it in milk and call us immediately.
  • Broken tooth: Rinse your mouth and save any pieces. Apply a cold compress to reduce swelling and contact us.
  • Lost filling or crown: Keep the area clean and avoid chewing on that side. Call us for a prompt appointment.
  • Abscess: This is a serious infection. Rinse with salt water and contact us immediately.
