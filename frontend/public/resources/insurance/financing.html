Financing Options

We understand that dental treatments can be a significant investment. That's why we offer various financing options to help make your dental care more affordable. Our goal is to ensure you can receive the dental care you need without financial stress.

CareCredit

CareCredit is a healthcare credit card that offers special financing options for dental care:

  • 0% interest for 6, 12, or 24 months (based on treatment amount)
  • Quick and easy application process
  • No annual fees
  • Can be used for future dental and medical expenses
  • Accepted at over 200,000 healthcare providers
Apply for CareCredit

Lending Club Patient Solutions

For larger treatment plans, we offer financing through Lending Club:

  • Fixed monthly payments
  • Terms from 24 to 84 months
  • Competitive interest rates
  • Quick approval process
  • No prepayment penalties

In-Office Payment Plans

We offer flexible in-office payment plans for patients who need to spread out their payments:

  • No interest charges
  • Customizable payment schedules
  • Automatic payment options available
  • No credit check required for amounts under $1,000
  • Convenient monthly payments
Contact Us

Health Savings Accounts (HSA) & Flexible Spending Accounts (FSA)

We accept payments from both HSA and FSA accounts:

  • Use pre-tax dollars for dental care
  • Save on your overall healthcare costs
  • Convenient payment options
  • Can be combined with other financing options

Important Information

  • All financing options are subject to approval
  • Interest rates and terms may vary based on credit history
  • Some financing options may require a minimum treatment amount
  • We recommend applying for financing before your treatment begins

To learn more about our financing options or to discuss a specific treatment plan, please contact our office. Our team will work with you to find the best solution for your needs and budget.
