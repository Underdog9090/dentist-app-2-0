Payment Plans & Options

We understand that dental care can be a significant investment. That's why we offer various payment options to make your dental care more affordable and accessible. Our goal is to ensure that cost is never a barrier to receiving the dental care you need.

In-Office Payment Plans

We offer flexible in-office payment plans for patients who need to spread out their payments:

  • No interest charges
  • Customizable payment schedules
  • Automatic payment options available
  • No credit check required for amounts under $1,000

CareCredit

We accept CareCredit, a healthcare credit card that offers special financing options:

  • 0% interest for 6, 12, or 24 months (based on treatment amount)
  • Quick and easy application process
  • No annual fees
  • Can be used for future dental and medical expenses

Lending Club Patient Solutions

For larger treatment plans, we offer financing through Lending Club:

  • Fixed monthly payments
  • Terms from 24 to 84 months
  • Competitive interest rates
  • Quick approval process

Discount Plans

We offer an in-house discount plan for patients without insurance:

  • Annual membership fee
  • Immediate savings on all dental services
  • No waiting periods
  • No annual maximums

Payment Methods Accepted

  • Cash
  • Credit Cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express, Discover)
  • Debit Cards
  • Personal Checks
  • Health Savings Accounts (HSA)
  • Flexible Spending Accounts (FSA)

To learn more about our payment options or to discuss a specific payment plan, please contact our office. Our team will work with you to find the best solution for your needs and budget.
