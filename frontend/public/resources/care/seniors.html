Senior Dental Care

  • Brush and floss daily to prevent gum disease and tooth loss.
  • Stay hydrated to avoid dry mouth, which can increase decay risk.
  • Clean dentures daily and remove them at night.
  • Visit your dentist regularly for oral cancer screenings.
  • Manage chronic conditions (like diabetes) that affect oral health.
  • Report any mouth sores, pain, or changes to your dentist promptly.

Good oral health is important at every age. We're here to help you keep your smile healthy!
