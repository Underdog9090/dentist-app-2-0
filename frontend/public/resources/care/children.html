Children's Dental Care

  • Begin brushing your child's teeth as soon as they appear.
  • Use a pea-sized amount of fluoride toothpaste for children over 3.
  • Supervise brushing until your child can do it well alone (around age 7-8).
  • Encourage healthy snacks like fruits and vegetables.
  • Schedule your child's first dental visit by their first birthday.
  • Ask your dentist about dental sealants and fluoride treatments.

Early dental care sets the foundation for a lifetime of healthy smiles!
